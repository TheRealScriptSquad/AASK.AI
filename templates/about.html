Our Project
"AASK - AI Analysis of Student Knowledge" revolutionizes academic forecasting through AI. By harnessing the power of machine learning, we've developed an innovative model that predicts student grades with remarkable accuracy. Users simply input various parameters, such as study habits, past performance, and extracurricular activities, through our intuitive frontend interface. Behind the scenes, our robust backend processes this data, leveraging advanced algorithms to generate personalized grade predictions. With AASK.AI, students can gain valuable insights into their academic journey, empowering them to make informed decisions and strive for excellence.
